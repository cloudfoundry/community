Nomination Statement: I am excited to stand for re-election to the Cloud Foundry TOC and continue serving the community that has been such an important part of my professional journey. Having already served two terms on the TOC, I have gained valuable experience helping guide the technical direction of the project while working alongside contributors, maintainers, end users, and ecosystem partners.

Cloud Foundry continues to evolve in a rapidly changing technology landscape. As we enter a new era shaped by AI, I believe Cloud Foundry has a unique opportunity to build on its strengths while embracing new possibilities. At the same time, cloud sovereignty has become a major consideration for many organizations, particularly in Europe, where regulatory requirements, data governance, and technological independence are driving strategic decisions. Cloud Foundry is well-positioned to help organizations balance innovation with control, portability, and operational consistency across diverse environments.

My focus remains on fostering a healthy, collaborative, and sustainable open-source ecosystem, supporting technical innovation, and ensuring Cloud Foundry continues to deliver value for organizations and developers alike. I am committed to listening to the community, encouraging constructive collaboration, and helping the TOC make thoughtful decisions that position the project for long-term success.

I would be honored to continue serving the Cloud Foundry community and appreciate your support.
