Nomination Statement: Matthew Kocher is a long-time leader for and contributor to the Cloud Foundry ecosystem. He presented at the original Cloud Foundry conference in 2013 and reflected on his experiences at the 2024 Cloud Foundry Summit, in addition to numerous other talks evangelizing Cloud Foundry.

Recently, he has served as an Approver for the Logging & Metrics and BOSH teams. He has been a leader in the move to adopting Open Telemetry for Cloud Foundry logs and metrics. Currently, he is driving the RFC to develop an Ubuntu 26.04 BOSH stemcell.

I'm excited to nominate Matthew, since he has been a personal mentor to me. He cares deeply about building technology to serve users' needs, whether they are application developers, platform operators, or anyone else who uses Cloud Foundry. He is passionate about fostering healthy and collaborative teams, in addition to a vibrant open source Cloud Foundry community.

I'm confident that, if elected to the TOC, Matthew will use his deep experience in the Cloud Foundry ecosystem and technical leadership to expertly guide the future of Cloud Foundry.
