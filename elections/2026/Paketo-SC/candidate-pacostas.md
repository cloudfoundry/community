Nomination Statement: I started contributing to Paketo Buildpacks by introducing the UBI Red Hat base builders. This required implementing the extension specification for the Node.js buildpacks and contributing patches to the Pack CLI. Since then, I have modified, and frequently refactored, the major workflows for buildpacks, composite buildpacks, stacks, and builders. My goal was to consolidate how Paketo Buildpacks builds and ships buildpacks and extensions, unifying the process based on the upstream philosophy from the github-config repository. I have also helped introduce the UBI 8, UBI 9, UBI 10, and Ubuntu Noble builders and base images, and I am currently working on introducing the Raccoon builders. Additionally, I helped implement multi-arch support for extensions, buildpacks, composite buildpacks, builders, and base images. To support these initiatives, I have also made several contributions to tooling, such as jam and occam.
Currently, I serve as a Maintainer for the Paketo Node.js buildpacks, Builders, Stacks, and tools. Having worked across so many foundational aspects of the project, I believe I have a strong grasp of the Paketo Buildpacks ecosystem and am well-prepared to help guide decision-making for its next steps.
